Chicken Pasta
Category: Meats
Time: 25 min
Heat: Medium

Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
200g pasta
1 tbsp oil
2 garlic cloves
100ml cream

Steps:
Prep chicken | No heat | 5 min | Slice and season the chicken.
Cook pasta | Medium | 10 min | Boil until tender.
Cook chicken | Medium-high | 8 min | Brown until cooked through.
Make sauce | Low | 5 min | Stir in garlic and cream.
Combine | Low | 2 min | Toss everything together and serve.
