🍳 Chef Knowledge

🥩 Steak

Pan: High heat until oil shimmers (slight smoke).

Why: High heat creates the Maillard reaction (crust).

Test: Drop of water should sizzle instantly.

Rare: 2 mins per side

Medium: 3–4 mins

Well: 5+ mins

Tip: Thickness changes time — thicker = longer.

Crust: deep brown, not grey

Edges: slight crisp

Press test: soft = rare, firm = well

Salt 30 mins before cooking

Pepper after searing

Butter + garlic last 2 mins

Pan not hot enough

Flipping too early

Not resting meat
